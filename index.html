Cinnamon Roll
Ingredients
Dough
  • 3 ½ cups flour
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 ¼ tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1 large egg
  • ⅓ cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp salt
Filling
  • ½ cup softened butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp cinnamon
Cream Cheese Frosting
  • 4 oz cream cheese (softened)
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
Instructions
  1. In a bowl, mix warm milk, sugar, and yeast. Let sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add egg, melted butter, and salt. Mix in the flour and knead until smooth. Cover and let rise for 1 hour.
  3. Mix brown sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl to make the filling.
  4. Roll the dough into a large rectangle. Spread softened butter on top and sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture.
  5. Roll tightly into a log and cut into 12 rolls.
  6. Place rolls in a baking pan, cover, and let rise 30 minutes.
  7. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 20–25 minutes or until golden.
  8. Beat cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla until smooth to make the frosting.
  9. Spread frosting over warm rolls and serve.
Garlic Butter Chicken
Ingredients
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 4 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp pepper
  • Fresh parsley (optional)
Instructions
  1. Season the chicken with paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Melt butter in a pan and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken and cook until golden on both sides.
  4. Simmer on low heat until chicken is fully cooked.
  5. Top with chopped parsley and serve warm.
